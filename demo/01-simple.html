  • Slide 1

    This is a deckard slide, inside a "set" of deckard slides.

    The "set" defines a standard transition between slides, which is why I slid in from the right, and I will slide out to the left. These directions reverse if you come back to this slide.

    You can click anywhere to go forward, or use the navigation links, or the arrow keys, or the "return" or "spacebar" keys. They should all be fairly intuitive.

  • Slide 2

    This is another deckard slide.

    It doesn't do much, but uses the same default transition as the first.

  • Slide 3

    This is another deckard slide.

    I have a different animation style to the default. The change in animation was specified declaritively in my a HTML by adding this attribute:
    data-deckard-animation="fall"

    Note the change in direction as you navigate into and out of this slide forwards and backwards.
